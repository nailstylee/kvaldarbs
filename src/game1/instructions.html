Adventure Fishing

Embark on a thrilling adventure to catch fish!

Instructions

Welcome to Adventure Fishing! Use the following controls to play:

  • Move the player: WASD
  • Switch rods: R
  • Cast bait: Hold and release left mouse button in the direction you wish to throw
  • Catch fish: use scroll wheel to move the bait closer to the player
  • Change how low the sinker is: use O and L to change the distance
